About Me

I’m a design technologist with a background in architecture, passionate about integrating emerging technologies into the built environment. With experience across Europe and Asia, I’ve led digital strategy, BIM implementation, and computational design on projects ranging from cultural landmarks to complex mixed-use developments. Whether mentoring teams, crafting automation pipelines, or exploring the potential of AI in design, my focus is always on elevating both process and product.
