About

Raised in Bombay. Studied architecture at AUB, in Bournemouth, UK. Then moved to Beijing, Barcelona, Hong Kong, London. Each city built a different layer.

In Beijing, worked as an Architectural Assistant and Designer across various practices, and co-founded de facto, a small design collective experimenting with 3D printing and digital fabrication, applying architectural thinking to fashion, food, and installations.

In Barcelona, studied at IAAC (the Institute for Advanced Architecture of Catalonia), focusing on computational design, smart materials, and advanced fabrication.

In Hong Kong, worked first at Enzyme APD, then at UNStudio. That is where the shift happened: from architect who uses technology to technologist who understands architecture.

In London, joined Benoy in 2022 and built the Design Technology function from zero. BIM infrastructure, ISO 19650 standards, and then VizAI, an AI visualisation platform now used across the practice globally. Today: Global Head of Design AI & Technology, running AI strategy and delivery across EMEA, measuring commercial impact, building the infrastructure that makes adoption self-sustaining.

Serves on the NLA Expert Panel on Built Environment Technology. Collaborates with IAAC Barcelona.
